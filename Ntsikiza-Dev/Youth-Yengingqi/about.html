About Youth Yengingqi

Physical Address

Youth Yengingqi (powered by G-Code) provides an online platform for the young kasi entrepreneurs / artists.

Here on this site we host and publish the youth's projects, be it events advetising posters or youth getherings such as activits i.e Youth Concious etc.

Due to the fast growing and merging of Tech and Entrepreneurship / Business, here on Youth Yengingqi we create tailor-made websites for their business ventures and we provide a space into our servers inoder to host for their business' websites.

It all started back in 2017 when Ntsikza the founder of the circle of young ambitious computer programmers and mobile app developers called G-Code, he saw how the young entrepreneurs lacked not only studios to craft their arts, start their businesses and publish themselves but failed to draw and create a packed customer base from various cape town townships.

With this in mind, his crew and him saw a slot where they can create websites for them at a lower price and provide a server to host their websites.
